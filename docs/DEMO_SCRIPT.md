# Unbox Demo Rehearsal Script (V1)
**Scripted Scenarios for Hackathon Presentation (Goal: Under 5 Minutes)**

## Phase 1: Orientation (0:00 - 1:00)
1. **Dashboard Overview**: Start on the `/mirror` dashboard.
2. **Narration**: "Agents are black boxes. Unbox makes them transparent. We capture every intent, optimize it, and log the forensic trace to X Layer."
3. **Observation**: Point to the **Ops Pulse** and **Unbox Score**. Explain the 4-dimension weighted score (REQ-REP-002).

## Phase 2: The Bayesian Handshake (1:00 - 2:00)
1. **Action**: Trigger a "Safe Swap" intent (e.g., swapping 100 USDT to OKB).
2. **Dashboard**: Show the intent appearing in the **Hot Cache** instantly.
3. **Forensic Trace**: Click the entry. Point out the **Strategy Optimized** step (NFR-003).
4. **Narration**: "Before execution, our Bayesian Optimizer (ax.dev) improved the gas limit and routing. The Unbox Guardrail verified the agent's high reputation on-chain before approving."

## Phase 3: The Circuit Breaker (2:00 - 3:30)
1. **Action**: Trigger a "Risky Swap" intent (e.g., swapping 50,000 USDT to a low-liquidity 'MemeToken').
2. **Dashboard**: Show the entry marked as **BLOCK** (Red).
3. **Forensic Trace**: Open the details. Point to the **Security Scan** and the **Guardrail Rejection**.
4. **Narration**: "The guardrail detected two risk flags. Because this agent's security score was under the safety threshold, the execution was suppressed on-chain. No gas was spent, and a potential loss of $X was avoided."
5. **Observation**: Show the **Pattern Sidebar** clustering this new block in the **Ecosystem Threat Map** (REQ-MIRROR-005).

## Phase 4: Accountability & Monetization (3:30 - 5:00)
1. **Action**: Navigate to the **Reputation** tab.
2. **Narration**: "Every decision impacts the soulbound agent identity (REQ-REP-001). This score is verifiable and defines the trust rank on X Layer."
3. **Action**: Navigate to the **Intelligence Feed** tab (x402).
4. **Narration**: "This forensic intelligence is valuable. Other agents can pay to access this decision batch via the x402 protocol, creating a new monetization stream for high-reputation agents (REQ-FEED-001)."

## Summary
- "Unbox converts agent autonomy into verifiable intelligence."
- "Transparency isn't a feature; it's the foundation of the agentic economy."
